Who are we?

The Coki Racing Team is a group of extraordinary students from different engineering fields and business administration. We participate in competitions hosted by the American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE), where team members must design and construct a small-scale vehicle that operates by chemical means.

Since 1999, the Coki Racing Team has proven to be one of the Chem-E-Car Competitions best participants. By implementing innovative and creative technologies, the team always manages to win a fraction of the awards. Our team is recognized both locally and nationally for our passion and success. Amongst our greatest accomplishments are several Regionals, two Nationals and one World Championship. We do not only build cars, we build people.
