Support Us

The Coki Racing Team is a special project of the Chemical Engineering Department of the Mayaguez University Campus (RUM), under the student chapter of the American Institute of Chemical Engineers (AIChE). Our participation in future competitions depends on the collection of the necessary funds to cover the expenses of approximately $7,700.00. This estimate includes the costs of registration, travel, diet, transportation and lodging. For this reason, we are requesting a donation from you We would be extremely grateful for the support you give to achieve our goals. Please contact us if you are interested in becoming a sponsor.
