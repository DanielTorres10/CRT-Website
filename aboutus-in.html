About us

Our History

The Coki Racing Team was founded in 1999 by Edwin Colon, who graduated from the University of Puerto Rico at Mayaguez with a bachelor Degree in Civil Engineering. Throughout the years, the Coki Racing Team has proved its success by winning a great number of awards and has managed to make two perfect runs, a feat only accomplished by few in the history of the Chem-E-Car Competition.
